Connecting Investors with Help

Description

A redesigned site based around a content-driven approach to getting investors connected with qualified help and the information to make the most of it

Deliverable

Full Site Wireframes & Mobile Design

Role

Information Architect & Designer

Technologies

Paper Prototypes, OmniGraffle & Sketch

Client

CFPB

Visit Site

We set out to capitalize on great content to help facilitate more connections between prospective clients and Certified Financial Planners. The new site was conceived to make the most of the traffic driven from paid social channels and to convert those users into in-person appointments.

At the center or our solution was the Learning Center, where users could self identify with a particular stage of investing that matched their current needs and could learn how to make the most of professional guidance. We reenforced CFPB's value proposition by crafting a user's experince in a way that helps them understand what makes the certification important.

I was brought in for initial information architecture, wireframing, and later for the mobile UI. This was a quick turn project which allowed the team to try some new things in some areas and to fall back to some tried-and-true processes that helped save time and resources. On the front end, I used paper prototyping to quickly turn key functionality into reusable modules. This allowed for easy iteration and collaboration to define the userflow, layouts, and overall structure of the site.
